# 19_ENTERPRISE_CLIENT_SYSTEM — Enterprise Alignment Protocols

## 1. Why This Exists & Why It Works
Enterprise web projects often encounter delays due to compliance audits, review cycles, and complex stakeholder alignments. This document establishes guidelines for managing enterprise client requirements. It works by setting clear presentation structures, compliance checklists, and review gates before deployment.

---

## 2. Review & Presentation Workflows
1. **Interactive Prototype Reviews**: Present designs using live interactive prototypes rather than static images. This speeds up design feedback and alignments.
2. **Detailed Performance Audits**: Share Lighthouse performance logs and accessibility scores early to clear security and compliance reviews.
3. **Structured Approval Workflows**: Secure visual sign-offs from key stakeholder teams (Art Director, Technical Director, Project Manager) before launching updates.

---

## 3. Compliance & Security Checklist
* **WCAG AA Compliance**: Confirm contrast ratios and keyboard navigation pathways.
* **Asset Optimization**: Deliver assets via high-performance CDNs and verify security headers are configured.

---

## 4. When to Use & Avoid
* **When to Use**: High-budget enterprise contracts, corporate platforms, public portals, and secure user panels.
* **When to Avoid**: Early visual concepts, personal portfolios, or small experimental projects.
