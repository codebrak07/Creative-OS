# 35_STORYTELLING_COMPONENT_SYSTEM — Storytelling Layout Specifications

## 1. Why This Exists & Why It Works
High-end portfolios and brands tell stories rather than showing standard feature grids. By pacing elements editorially, luxury sites create premium spaces where milestones, origins, and craftsmanship are showcased. This document details layout rules for chronological timelines, step-by-step galleries, and splits.

---

## 2. Core Rules & Guidelines
1. **Pacing Milestones**: Story elements (milestones or steps) must occupy generous vertical paddings and snap positions.
2. **Alternating Split Blocks**: Content layout must alternate between asset-left/copy-right and asset-right/copy-left, aligning details to a strict typographic line grid.
3. **No Overlapping Captions**: Ensure that detail labels and dates use clean monospace typography and have fixed layouts that remain legible on mobile portrait screens.

---

## 3. Implementation Details
* **HeritageTimeline**: A timeline detailing history milestones with visual and descriptive entries.
* **CraftsmanshipSection**: Editorial sections focusing on craft steps with interactive visual step indices.
