# Award Submission Guide
## Where and how to submit for maximum visibility

### Awwwards (awwwards.com)
**Submission cost:** $29 (single) / $49 (with mobile review)
**Best time to submit:** Tuesday–Thursday. Avoid December/summer.
**What they want:**
- Live URL (not staging, not password-protected)
- Site description: what it does, not how impressive it is. 2–3 sentences.
- Tags: be specific (WebGL, Three.js, GSAP, React, etc.)
- Screenshots: 1440px desktop + mobile. Crisp. Taken in full-page screenshot.
- Mobile submission: worth the extra $20 — separate mobile score

**Description formula that works:**
"[Client/project name] is [what it does] for [who]. Built with [key tech].
The experience [one sentence about the core interaction]."

**Do NOT write:** "An immersive digital experience that pushes the boundaries..."

---

### FWA — Favourite Website Awards (thefwa.com)
**Submission cost:** Free
**Turnaround:** 24–72 hours
**FWA of the Day → FWA of the Month pipeline**
Getting FWA + Awwwards simultaneously (like Lempens) = maximum visibility

---

### CSS Design Awards (cssdesignawards.com)
**Submission cost:** Free (basic) / $19 (featured review)
**Good for:** getting initial traction, smaller jury = faster turnaround
**Pro tip:** submit here first to test scores before Awwwards

---

### Mindsparkle Magazine (mindsparklemag.com)
**Submission cost:** Free
**What they look for:** editorial, minimal, typographic excellence
**Audience:** designers and studios. Great for agency work visibility.

---

### Orpetron (orpetron.com)
**Submission cost:** Free
**Niche:** animation-forward, motion-first sites
**Good for:** When the signature technique is the story

---

### SiteInspire (siteinspire.com)
**Submission cost:** Free
**Curation:** tight — they reject most submissions
**What they like:** restraint, editorial, Bauhaus-influenced, minimal

---

### Httpster (httpster.net)
**Submission cost:** Free
**Good for:** early buzz, high traffic source

---

### Godly (godly.website)
**Submission cost:** Free / Paid Express Review
**Curation:** Extremely strict, design-driven interactive websites.
**Why it matters:** Godly often drives *more* developer/designer referral traffic than an actual Awwwards SOTD win. It is a permanent archive of premium UI design.

---

### Muzli (muzli.in)
**Submission cost:** Free (submit via their online form or Chrome Extension submission hub)
**Curation:** Hand-picked by the Muzli editorial team.
**Why it matters:** As a default browser tab extension for millions of design professionals, a "Muzli Pick" or home page feature delivers massive immediate traffic spikes.

---

### Lapa Ninja (lapa.ninja)
**Submission cost:** Free / Paid Express
**Curation:** Clean, conversion-focused, and premium landing page layouts.
**Why it matters:** Highly relevant for AI SaaS platforms, luxury commercial products, and structured agency portfolios.

---

### Land-book (land-book.com)
**Submission cost:** Free / Paid Express
**Curation:** High-quality landing pages, portfolios, templates, and blog designs.
**Why it matters:** Excellent long-tail SEO referral traffic for design and development services.

---

### Best Website Gallery (bestwebsite.gallery)
**Submission cost:** Free / Paid submission
**Curation:** Curated list of premium websites, filtered by style, color, and technology.
**Why it matters:** Running since 2008, it is a highly trusted inspiration archive with high authority and permanent backlinks.

---

## Submission Sequence for Maximum Impact

Day 0 (launch day):
1. Submit to CSS Design Awards (fastest review — within 24h)
2. Submit to FWA (24–72h)
3. Submit to Awwwards (same day)
4. Submit to Mindsparkle, Orpetron, Httpster (free, quick)

Day 1–3: Share on social
- Dribbble: post 4 best screenshots + 1 short interaction video (GIF or MP4)
- LinkedIn: project post with tech stack breakdown (devs share these)
- Twitter/X: tag @awwwards, @thefwa, @cssdesignawards
- Instagram: behind-the-scenes process reel (Studio account)

Week 1: Outreach & Curation Submission
- Share directly with: CSS-Tricks, Codrops, Designmodo if technique is novel
- Submit to: Godly (godly.website) for premium interactive archiving
- Submit to: Muzli (via submission tab/form)
- Submit to: Lapa Ninja & Land-book (for landing page / design grid search index)
- Submit to: Best Website Gallery (BWG)
- Add to: Bestfolios if it's a portfolio
